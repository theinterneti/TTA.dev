## Creating Guides

### Principles

‚úÖ **Start with the user's goal** - What do they want to achieve?
‚úÖ **Show complete examples** - Working code, not snippets
‚úÖ **Explain why, not just how** - Context matters
‚úÖ **Test all examples** - Ensure they work
‚úÖ **Link to related content** - Connect the knowledge graph

### Writing Tips

1. **Use clear headings** - Break into digestible sections
2. **Include prerequisites** - What users need to know
3. **Show expected output** - So users can verify
4. **Explain error cases** - What can go wrong
5. **Provide next steps** - Continue learning
